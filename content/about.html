It was always about agency.

Back to one.

2012 Started solo, in Goa

1 Back to one — building, not managing

Fifteen years ago, I started with a simple principle: agency. It's why I named the studio Ajency.in. I began as a solo product consultant, moved to Goa, worked closely with founders I believed in, and built a studio around that idea.

About Ajency.in

The studio: a Goa-based software development and UI/UX design company, founded in 2012. We did product engineering, interface design, and web & ecommerce work — mostly for startups, including teams backed by Sequoia, Tiger Global, and Google Capital.

As the team grew to 50, some of that agency got lost. We always joked we were “buzzword compliant.” Funnily enough, agency itself has now become a buzzword — thanks to AI agents.

The acquisition

After the 2022 acquisition, I spent three years at Turtlemint, a fintech that recently IPO'd. Turtlemint is primarily an insurance distribution business; I was product lead for new verticals — mutual funds and lending — and, horizontally, product lead for data, automation, and AI across the company. Then I stepped out of full-time work entirely.

On the acquisition

Turtlemint had been a client since 2021. After a couple of years working together, they offered to acquire the studio — an acqui-hire, for the team and the talent. They made sure I had no good reason to say no. :)

What I didn't expect was how much that step would give back. Two decades of payroll, deadlines, and stakeholder management had quietly drained something I only later had a name for: cognitive space. Once it returned, I started following curiosity again — going deep on things that interested me, dropping the ones that didn't, without needing every interest to justify itself.

Now

One of those itches was building things again.

I got AI-pilled, spent weeks inside Claude Code, and took an automation project from discovery to delivery for an automobile dealer. Solo. Turns out I love working solo — building, not managing and delegating like the last 20 years.

So Ajency.in 2.0 isn't a comeback. It's what the space made room for. More of the same, and yet very different. Slower. Calmer. Curiosity-led — I take on work I genuinely want to think about. More time building, less time managing.

Agency, in every sense:
For AI agents learning autonomy.
For clients building with intention.
And for myself — still figuring out what work and freedom look like together.

Hit me up if you want to chat about AI agents, technology, products, design — or even bike trips and cocktails.
