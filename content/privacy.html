Privacy

The short version: this site collects almost nothing.

No cookies. No ads, no trackers, no cookie banner needed.

Analytics. Pageviews are counted by GoatCounter, which stores no cookies and no personal identifiers — just that a page was viewed.

Subscribing. If you subscribe, your email address is stored with EmailOctopus, the service that sends the emails. It’s used for one thing: telling you when a new post is up. Every email has an unsubscribe link; or write to me and I’ll delete it.

Emailing me. If you email me, I have your email address. I use it to reply.

Questions: talktous@ajency.in
