Pairs reading-column prose with margin content (a Digression or MarginFig). Wide screens put the aside in the right margin; narrow screens flow it inline. Keep the aside shorter than the prose it rides with. Inside an Essay only.

```tsx
<Aside aside={<Digression summary="About Ajency.in">The studio: founded 2012 in Goa…</Digression>}>
  <p>Fifteen years ago, I started with a simple principle: agency.</p>
</Aside>
```
